about me

my name is Alexis Ang!

i'm a class of 2020 graduate from the university of california, davis (go aggies) with a bachelor's degree in statistics and a minor in english. after graduating, i was hired as a professional writer and editor, but i quickly found the profession to be unfulfilling. i've spent a majority of my educational career interested in technology, the arts, and the intersection between these two subjects, and i feel lucky enough to have found a passion for ux and ui design that suits my interests perfectly. i'm inspired by aesthetic, functional, and visually appealing designs and am excited to demonstrate my creative skills in a professional setting.

outside of ux/ui design, my interests include collecting artistic hobbies (namely painting, crocheting, sewing, drawing, and sculpting), being overly critical of movies, thrift shopping, fencing, and cats.
